About MEDIK-PLUS PHARMACY

Empowering our community with accessible, high-quality, professional pharmacy and wellness services.

Our Journey

Dedicated to Clinical Precision and Patient Care

Established in 2006, MEDIK-PLUS PHARMACY has grown from a local apothecary to a state-of-the-art clinical pharmacy. We are driven by a single mission: to integrate professional medical consultations with convenient, high-quality pharmaceutical services.

We pride ourselves on offering a wide range of services beyond standard prescriptions. Our modern clinic features private patient consulting rooms, diagnostic assessment spaces, and an advanced temperature-controlled facility to preserve all medications.

We work collaboratively with local healthcare providers to synchronize prescription renewals and manage chronic care, ensuring a seamless treatment plan for every patient.
